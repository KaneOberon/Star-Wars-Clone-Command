Clone Troopers Battalions

Explore the key battalions of the Grand Army of the Republic, their commanders, and their roles in the Clone Wars.

212th Attack Battalion

Commander: Clone Commander Cody

Known for their loyalty and tactical discipline, the 212th fought alongside Jedi General Obi-Wan Kenobi in numerous battles, including the Battle of Utapau.

501st Legion

Commander: Captain Rex

Also called "Vader's Fist," the 501st was an elite unit that served under Anakin Skywalker, later Darth Vader. They were pivotal in many major conflicts during the Clone Wars.

104th Battalion (Wolfpack)

Commander: Clone Commander Wolffe

Distinguished by their unique wolf-themed helmets, the 104th were known for their resilience and played a key role in campaigns on planets such as Ryloth.

Doom Company

Commander: Commander Doom

Doom Company was a specialized unit of commandos tasked with covert and high-risk operations during the Clone Wars.

Galactic Marines

Commander: Bacara

The Galactic Marines were an elite heavy infantry unit known for their durability and combat skills in harsh environments.

Coruscant Guard

Commander: Commander Fox

The Coruscant Guard acted as the Republic’s elite security force, responsible for law enforcement and protecting key political figures on Coruscant.
