  • 22 BBY — First Battle of Geonosis:
    The Clone Wars begin. Jedi Master Mace Windu leads over 200 Jedi to rescue Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin Skywalker, and Padmé Amidala. Clone troopers under Yoda's command engage Separatist forces. This marks the activation of the Grand Army of the Republic.
  • 21.9 BBY — Battle of Kamino:
    Separatist forces launch a surprise assault on Tipoca City. ARC troopers Fives and Echo lead the defense alongside Jedi Generals Shaak Ti and Anakin Skywalker. Clones successfully repel the invasion, preserving Kaminoan cloning facilities.
  • 21.8 BBY — Battle of Christophis:
    Jedi Generals Obi-Wan Kenobi and Anakin Skywalker lead the 501st Legion in a fierce defense of Christophis. They encounter Admiral Trench and later gain support from Ahsoka Tano, recently assigned to Anakin.
  • 21.7 BBY — Malevolence Crisis:
    General Grievous’s flagship, the Malevolence, threatens Republic fleets with its ion cannon. Jedi Master Plo Koon and his Wolfpack are ambushed. With help from Anakin and Ahsoka, the Malevolence is ultimately destroyed.
  • 21.6 BBY — Battle of Ryloth:
    Jedi Generals Mace Windu, Obi-Wan Kenobi, and Anakin Skywalker liberate the Twi'lek homeworld. The 212th, 501st, and Waxer & Boil's squad play vital roles in dismantling the Separatist blockade and freeing hostages.
  • 21.5 BBY — Second Battle of Geonosis:
    Jedi Generals Luminara Unduli, Ki-Adi-Mundi, and Anakin Skywalker lead the 501st and other clone divisions in retaking Geonosis. The battle includes intense combat in a droid factory and the introduction of Jedi Padawan Barriss Offee.
  • 21.2 BBY — Citadel Rescue Mission:
    The Jedi and elite clone troopers (including ARC troopers and the 501st) infiltrate the Citadel prison to rescue Jedi Master Even Piell. The mission, coordinated by Anakin, Obi-Wan, Ahsoka, and Captain Tarkin, suffers heavy losses.
  • 21 BBY — Battle of Mon Cala:
    Jedi Generals Kit Fisto and Anakin Skywalker lead underwater clone troopers to stabilize Mon Cala. They defend the Mon Calamari monarchy against Separatist-backed Quarren insurgents with help from Prince Lee-Char.
  • 20.5 BBY — Battle of Umbara:
    The 501st, under temporary command of Jedi General Pong Krell, engage in a brutal campaign on Umbara. His controversial tactics cause unrest. Captain Rex leads a mutiny, and Krell is arrested and executed for treason.
  • 20.1 BBY — Zygerrian Slave Conflict:
    Anakin, Obi-Wan, and Ahsoka uncover a Zygerrian slaving operation linked to the Separatists. They work to free captured Togrutas and destroy the slaver network.
  • 20 BBY — Attack on the Jedi Temple Hangars:
    A bombing within the Jedi Temple leads to an investigation that strains Ahsoka’s relationship with the Order. She is framed and later leaves the Jedi, disillusioned by their lack of trust.
  • 19.5 BBY — Battle of Scipio:
    The Banking Clan headquarters is attacked. Jedi General Anakin Skywalker and Captain Clovis uncover corruption. Clovis dies, and the Republic assumes control of the banks, centralizing war funding.
  • 19.1 BBY — Siege of Mandalore:
    Ahsoka Tano leads a battalion of the 501st alongside Captain Rex to capture Darth Maul. The operation is independent of the Republic and is concurrent with events on Coruscant. Maul is defeated and taken prisoner.
  • 19 BBY — Order 66:
    Chancellor Palpatine executes Order 66. Clone troopers across the galaxy turn on their Jedi commanders. Ahsoka and Rex narrowly escape during a harrowing escape from a crashing Star Destroyer. The Republic collapses, and the Empire is born.
